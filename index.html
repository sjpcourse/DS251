Content

About

Welcome to the course on Artificial Intelligence! This comprehensive and engaging course explores the fundamental concepts and techniques that drive the development of intelligent systems and their applications in solving complex problems. Whether you're intrigued by artificial intelligence, curious about automated reasoning, or interested in game-playing algorithms, this course will provide you with a solid foundation in these areas.

Syllabus

  • Introduction : Course Introduction, Motivation.
  • Problem solving by search : State Space, Problem Reduction, Graph Search, Game Playing.
  • Automated Reasoning : Propositional and first order logic, inference and deduction, resolution refutation
  • Planning : State-space, plan space and partial order planning, planning algorithms.
  • Reasoning under uncertainty : Probabilistic reasoning, belief networks.
  • Reinforcement Learning : MDP, Policy Search, Q-Learning, Applications.
